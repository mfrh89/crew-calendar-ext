How to find your calendar URL

Crew Calendar uses a private ICS link to read your events. Here's how to get it.

📅

Google Calendar

  1. Open Google Calendar in your browser (calendar.google.com)
  2. In the left sidebar, hover over the calendar you want to add and click the three dots (⋮)
  3. Click "Settings and sharing"
  4. Scroll down to "Integrate calendar"
  5. Copy the link under "Secret address in iCal format"
https://calendar.google.com/calendar/ical/youraddress%40gmail.com/private-xxxxx/basic.ics
⚠️ Use the Secret address, not the public one — the secret address includes your private events.
🍎

Apple Calendar iCloud

  1. Open Calendar on your Mac
  2. Right-click the calendar you want to share in the left sidebar
  3. Click "Share Calendar…"
  4. Enable "Public Calendar"
  5. Click "Copy Link" — this gives you a webcal:// URL
  6. Replace webcal:// with https:// before pasting into Crew Calendar
https://p01-caldav.icloud.com/published/2/yourlink.ics
⚠️ A public iCloud calendar is visible to anyone with the link. Only share calendars that don't contain sensitive personal information.
📱

Apple Calendar iPhone / iPad

  1. Open Settings → Calendar → Accounts
  2. Tap your iCloud account → Calendar
  3. Alternatively, open the Calendar app → tap Calendars at the bottom
  4. Tap the ⓘ next to the calendar → Share Calendar
  5. Enable Public Calendar and tap Share Link
  6. Replace webcal:// with https:// before using
